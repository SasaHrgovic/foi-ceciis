Travel

How to get in Varaždin and find us - useful travel information

TRAVEL BY PLANE

Zagreb International Airport

Ulica Rudolfa Fizira 1

10410 Velika Gorica

Phone: +385 1 4562 222

More information: http://www.zagreb-airport.hr/

Zagreb International Airport provides direct line (bus shuttle) to Zagreb. The bus terminal of Pleso prijevoz company provides direct line (bus shuttle) to and from Airport. Shuttle terminal is located at the ground floor of the Central bus station. The rent-a-car service or taxy service are also available.


Pleso prijevoz

Avenija Marina Držića 4

10000 Zagreb

Phone: +385 1 6331 999

More information:http://www.plesoprijevoz.hr/index.html

Once you get to Zagreb you can travel to Varaždin by train or by bus. Information about travel arrangements can be provided by Central bus station Zagreb or Central train station Zagreb.

CECIIS 2016

Faculty of Organization and Informatics

September 21, 2016 – September 23, 2016

The aim of the Central European Conference on Information and Intelligent Systems (CECIIS) is to promote the interface of researchers involved in the development and application of methods and techniques in the field of information and intelligent systems.
